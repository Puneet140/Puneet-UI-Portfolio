Work

ShopEase – E-commerce UI Design

1. Project Overview

ShopEase is a modern e-commerce platform designed to create a smooth and intuitive online shopping experience. The project focuses on improving product discovery, simplifying the buying process, and providing a clean and user-friendly interface.

2. Problem

Many e-commerce websites suffer from cluttered interfaces, confusing navigation, and complicated checkout processes, making it difficult for users to quickly find and purchase products. Many e-commerce users face difficulties while shopping for clothing because they are unsure about their correct size. This often leads to incorrect purchases and product returns. To address this issue, the platform includes a “Check Your Size” feature, allowing users to enter their measurements and find the most suitable size before purchasing.

3. Goal

The goal of ShopEase was to design a simple, visually clean, and efficient shopping experience where users can easily browse products, check sizes, and add items to their cart.

4. Target Users

  • Online shoppers looking for fashion products
  • Users who want quick and simple shopping experiences
  • Mobile and desktop users

5. User Flow

Login / Sign Up -> OTP Verification -> Homepage -> Browse Categories -> View Product -> Add to Cart -> Cart Review

6. Wireframes

7. UI Design

8. Key Features

  • Login and Signup with OTP Verification
  • Product browsing by category
  • Product Tiles with image and description
  • Size checker feature
  • Add-to-cart functionality
  • Cart management system

ShopEase – E-commerce UI Design

1. Project Overview

Mr Designer is a graphic design startup website created to showcase creative design services and portfolio work. The project focuses on presenting branding, graphic design, and digital creative services through a modern and visually engaging interface.

2. Problem

Many small design studios struggle to present their work professionally online. Their websites often lack clear structure, visual hierarchy, and engaging portfolio presentation, making it difficult for potential clients to explore their services.

3. Goal

The goal of this project was to design a clean and visually appealing website that allows a design studio to showcase its services, portfolio, and creative work in a professional way while ensuring easy navigation for users.

4. Target Users

  • Businesses looking for graphic design services
  • Clients seeking branding and creative solutions
  • Startups needing logo and visual identity design

5. User Flow

Home -> Explore Services -> View Portfolio -> Learn about us -> Contact / Hire Designer
